About Me

I'm Paul, an artist based in Cardiff, South Wales. I've always been creative and throughout education found myself gravitating towards subjects that allowed me the freedom to express this. I found I had a special connection with art and spent most of my time focusing on refining skills and techniques and finding the styles and mediums that resulted in works I was most proud of. I've previously worked on commissioned pieces for clients and have also used artwork as gifts for family and friends' special occasions (usually when money is tight,) but above all else, I take every opportunity possible because I love to create.

My Work

I work best somewhere in the cross section of realism, mark making and mathematical design. I'm proficient in digital art and graphic design alongside multiple painting disciplines but I find my greatest results come from the tried and trusted combination of pencil and paper. I like to work in high contrast and so tend to find myself gravitating towards working in black and white with the occasional coloured piece or colour pop in an otherwise greyscale work. My inspirations come mainly from portraiture, using source materials of film, television and graphic novels as well as real life subjects. I hope you enjoy this site dedicated to my work and I encourage you to contact me with any and all questions on commissions, existing works or anything else!
